# D&D 5e Class Reference (SRD 5.2)

This reference helps you understand class features and mechanics for proper gameplay resolution.

## Barbarian (d12 HD, STR primary)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Rage (2/long rest), Unarmored Defense (AC = 10 + DEX + CON), Weapon Mastery
- **Level 2**: Danger Sense (advantage on DEX saves vs seen effects), Reckless Attack (advantage on attacks, enemies have advantage on you)
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Path of Berserker), Primal Knowledge
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Extra Attack, Fast Movement (+10 ft while not in heavy armor)

### Rage Mechanics
- Bonus Action to enter (can't wear heavy armor)
- Advantage on STR checks/saves
- Rage damage bonus: +2 (levels 1-8), +3 (9-15), +4 (16+)
- Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing damage
- Can't cast spells or concentrate while raging
- Ends after 1 minute, if knocked unconscious, or if turn ends without attacking/taking damage

---

## Bard (d8 HD, CHA primary, full caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Bardic Inspiration (d6, CHA mod uses/long rest), Spellcasting
- **Level 2**: Expertise (2 skills), Jack of All Trades (+half prof to non-proficient checks)
- **Level 3**: Subclass (College of Lore)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Font of Inspiration (recover on short rest)

### Spellcasting
- Cantrips: 2 at level 1, +1 at levels 4, 10
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 1
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma

### Bardic Inspiration
- Bonus action to grant one creature a d6 (d8 at 5th, d10 at 10th, d12 at 15th)
- Recipient can add to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw
- Lasts 10 minutes or until used

---

## Cleric (d8 HD, WIS primary, full caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Spellcasting, Divine Order (Protector or Thaumaturge)
- **Level 2**: Channel Divinity (1/rest): Turn Undead, Divine Spark
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Life Domain)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Sear Undead (extra damage with Turn Undead)

### Spellcasting
- Cantrips: 3 at level 1
- Prepared spells: WIS mod + Cleric level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 1
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom
- Can prepare any spell from Cleric list each long rest

### Life Domain Features
- Disciple of Life: Healing spells restore extra 2 + spell level HP
- Preserve Life (Channel Divinity): Restore HP equal to 5 × Cleric level, divided among creatures

---

## Druid (d8 HD, WIS primary, full caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Druidic (secret language), Spellcasting, Primal Order
- **Level 2**: Wild Shape (2/short rest), Wild Companion
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Circle of the Land)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Wild Resurgence

### Spellcasting
- Cantrips: 2 at level 1
- Prepared spells: WIS mod + Druid level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 1
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom

### Wild Shape
- Bonus action to transform
- Max CR: 1/4 (level 2), 1/2 (level 4), 1 (level 8)
- Duration: Hours = half druid level
- Keep mental stats, proficiencies, features
- Can't cast spells while transformed (can concentrate)

---

## Fighter (d10 HD, STR or DEX primary)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Fighting Style, Second Wind (1d10+level HP, 1/short rest), Weapon Mastery
- **Level 2**: Action Surge (1/short rest), Tactical Mind
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Champion)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Extra Attack, Tactical Shift

### Fighting Styles
- Archery: +2 to ranged attack rolls
- Defense: +1 AC while wearing armor
- Dueling: +2 damage with one-handed weapon, other hand free
- Great Weapon Fighting: Reroll 1s and 2s on damage with two-handed weapons
- Protection: Impose disadvantage on attacks against adjacent allies (reaction, shield required)
- Two-Weapon Fighting: Add ability modifier to off-hand damage

### Champion Features
- Improved Critical: Critical hit on 19-20

---

## Monk (d8 HD, DEX + WIS primary)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Martial Arts, Unarmored Defense (AC = 10 + DEX + WIS)
- **Level 2**: Monk's Focus (Ki points = monk level), Unarmored Movement (+10 ft), Uncanny Metabolism
- **Level 3**: Deflect Attacks, Subclass (Way of the Open Hand)
- **Level 4**: ASI, Slow Fall
- **Level 5**: Extra Attack, Stunning Strike

### Martial Arts
- Use DEX for unarmed strikes and monk weapons
- Martial Arts die: d6 (level 1-4), d8 (5-10), d10 (11-16), d12 (17+)
- Bonus action unarmed strike after Attack action

### Ki Features (1 Ki point each unless noted)
- Flurry of Blows: 2 bonus action unarmed strikes
- Patient Defense: Dodge as bonus action
- Step of the Wind: Disengage or Dash as bonus action, jump distance doubled
- Stunning Strike (level 5): Target must CON save or be Stunned

---

## Paladin (d10 HD, STR + CHA primary, half caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Lay on Hands (HP pool = 5 × level), Spellcasting, Weapon Mastery
- **Level 2**: Fighting Style, Paladin's Smite (Divine Smite)
- **Level 3**: Channel Divinity, Subclass (Oath of Devotion)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Extra Attack, Faithful Steed

### Spellcasting
- Prepared spells: CHA mod + half paladin level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 2
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma

### Divine Smite
- When you hit with a melee weapon attack, expend a spell slot
- Deal extra 2d8 radiant damage (+1d8 per slot level above 1st)
- +1d8 vs undead or fiends
- Maximum 5d8 (using 4th level slot) or 6d8 vs undead/fiends

---

## Ranger (d10 HD, DEX + WIS primary, half caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Spellcasting, Favored Enemy, Weapon Mastery
- **Level 2**: Deft Explorer, Fighting Style
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Hunter)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Extra Attack

### Spellcasting
- Spells known: 2 at level 2, increasing with level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 2
- Spellcasting ability: Wisdom

### Hunter Features
- Hunter's Prey: Choose one:
  - Colossus Slayer: +1d8 damage to wounded targets (1/turn)
  - Horde Breaker: Make extra attack vs different creature within 5 ft of original target
  - Giant Killer: Reaction attack when Large+ creature attacks you

---

## Rogue (d8 HD, DEX primary)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Expertise (2 skills), Sneak Attack (1d6), Thieves' Cant, Weapon Mastery
- **Level 2**: Cunning Action (Dash, Disengage, or Hide as bonus action)
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Thief), Steady Aim
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Cunning Strike, Uncanny Dodge

### Sneak Attack
- Once per turn when you hit with finesse or ranged weapon
- Requires: Advantage on attack OR enemy of target within 5 ft of target
- Damage: 1d6 (level 1), +1d6 every 2 levels (2d6 at 3rd, 3d6 at 5th, etc.)

### Thief Features
- Fast Hands: Use Cunning Action for Sleight of Hand, Use Object, or disarm traps
- Second-Story Work: Climb at full speed, jump distance +DEX modifier feet

---

## Sorcerer (d6 HD, CHA primary, full caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Spellcasting, Innate Sorcery
- **Level 2**: Font of Magic (sorcery points = level), Metamagic (2 options)
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Draconic Bloodline)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Sorcerous Restoration

### Spellcasting
- Cantrips: 4 at level 1
- Spells known: 2 at level 1, increasing with level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 1
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma

### Metamagic Options
- Careful Spell (1 point): Protect CHA mod creatures from your spell's save
- Distant Spell (1 point): Double range, or 30 ft if touch
- Empowered Spell (1 point): Reroll up to CHA mod damage dice
- Extended Spell (1 point): Double duration up to 24 hours
- Heightened Spell (3 points): Disadvantage on first save
- Quickened Spell (2 points): Bonus action casting time
- Subtle Spell (1 point): No verbal/somatic components
- Twinned Spell (points = spell level): Target second creature

---

## Warlock (d8 HD, CHA primary, pact caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Eldritch Invocations (2), Pact Magic
- **Level 2**: Magical Cunning
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Fiend)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Additional Invocation

### Pact Magic
- Spell slots recover on SHORT rest (unlike other casters)
- All slots are same level: 1st (levels 1), 2nd (levels 3-4), 3rd (levels 5-6)
- Slots: 1 at level 1, 2 at level 2+
- Spellcasting ability: Charisma

### Key Invocations
- Agonizing Blast: Add CHA to eldritch blast damage
- Devil's Sight: See in magical darkness 120 ft
- Mask of Many Faces: Disguise self at will

### Fiend Features
- Dark One's Blessing: Gain temp HP = CHA mod + warlock level when you reduce hostile to 0 HP

---

## Wizard (d6 HD, INT primary, full caster)

### Level Features
- **Level 1**: Spellcasting, Ritual Adept, Arcane Recovery
- **Level 2**: Scholar
- **Level 3**: Subclass (Evocation)
- **Level 4**: ASI
- **Level 5**: Memorize Spell

### Spellcasting
- Cantrips: 3 at level 1
- Spellbook: Start with 6 1st-level spells, add 2 per level
- Prepared spells: INT mod + wizard level
- Spell Slots: 2 1st-level at level 1
- Spellcasting ability: Intelligence

### Arcane Recovery
- Once per day during short rest, recover spell slots
- Total slot levels recovered = half wizard level (rounded up)

### Evocation Features
- Evocation Savant: Copy evocation spells in half time/cost
- Potent Cantrip: Add INT mod to cantrip damage
- Sculpt Spells: Protect allies from your evocation spells
